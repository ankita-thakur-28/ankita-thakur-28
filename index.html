Approach

I keep test suites simple and readable: clear structure, single responsibility tests, and meaningful assertions. I prefer end-to-end tests for high-value flows and rely on API/unit tests for fast feedback. In each project I add logging, screenshots on failure, and retries only where appropriate to avoid masking real issues.
